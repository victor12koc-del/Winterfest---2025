Guidelines & Rules

  • Each campus can send a maximum of 50 students.
  • Students taking part in cultural events and cricket may participate in all other events as well.
  • No overlaps allowed for participants in: club events and sports (badminton, basketball and football).
  • Students are allowed to participate in only 2 sports events.
  • In case of clash in sports events timing, participants can choose the one they wish to be a part of.
  • All campuses are requested to reach XIME Kochi on 28th November.
  • Hotel rooms will be available from 12 PM on 27th to 12 PM on 28th November.
  • Students must check out before leaving for XIME Kochi campus on the morning of the 28th.
  • Shuttle buses from hotel to campus will operate from 7:30 AM to 8:10 AM on 28th November.
